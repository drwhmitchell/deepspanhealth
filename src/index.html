What Are Health Navigation Systems?

At DeepSpan Health we are pioneers of Health Navigation Systems: platforms that assist you in self-evaluating your health in different areas such as sleep, diet and disease-prevention and then enabling you to make the best choices among different health product choices. We're like a compass for health product choices! Trying to address your insomnia by purchasing various suppliments or perhaps a mattress-cooler? Our technology makes it easy to first assess your real needs, then match those against solutions proven to work by others in our community whose health characteristics match yours.


Key Technologies

Deep Biometric Sensor Platforms

Broad multi-sensor platform automates the collection of health data from multiple sources

AI-Based Health Evaluation

Health data sources combine into "healthprint" patterns which are analyzed and compared

Personalized Recommendations

Custom health analysis and recommendations lead to more accurate, personalized solutions

Community-Sourced Efficacy

Community solution trials enable efficacy comparisons with those of similar healthprints
